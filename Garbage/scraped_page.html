Government Arts College Highlights 2024

Located in Dausa, Rajasthan, Government Arts College was established in 2016. Government Arts College, a leading institute in higher education, offers Degree courses to students. These courses are provided at the UG and PG level across Humanities & Social Sciences stream. The institute offers courses in Full Time mode. The institute is famous for offering courses such as B.A. and M.A. With this institute, students can enjoy studying in the state of the art infrastructure.
